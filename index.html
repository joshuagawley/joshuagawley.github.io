Joshua Gawley is a British composer currently based in Bristol, where he is studying at the University of Bristol.

Alongside composing choral music, Joshua sings in the University Chamber Choir and University Church Choir. He also runs a successful YouTube channel where he uploads score videos, mainly focusing on 20th and 21st century choral music. His other interests including winding up his twin brother Nicholas and occasionally studying maths.
